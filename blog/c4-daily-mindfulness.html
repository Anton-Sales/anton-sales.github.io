Daily Mindfulness

What did you learn from your 'mindfulness conversation'? How did the other person find it?

My Dad and I practiced mindfulness conversation together. In our conversation, I have realised that my Dad is a great listener. I've always thought that my dad didn't have much to say but I now realised that he is just great listener. I learned from our mindful conversation that it takes time and patience to be a great listener.

Which of the three suggested techniques will you use to sustain your practice?

I will sustain the 'walking meditation' as it interested me the most. Often, I catch myself staring into abyss, and regaining attention like a snap of a finger. I would incorporate walking meditation as it will keep my mind active and conscious throughout the day.
