Meditation Process

How did the 'process over product' concept affect the way you tackled the site redesign and rebuild?

It affected me in a positive way because it gave me the ability of creativity. Without looking at the end product and building within the framework improved the way I researched and gave me a better understanding of my project. They are still parts of the website that I’m still trying to wrap my head around, but will soon be understood overtime.

What did you think about meditation before reading chapter 2?

I haven’t really thought much about meditation other than another form of exercise. I see it as another practice to improve one’s mind and well-being. After trying it several times since reading chapter 1, I’ve noticed I gained improvement on patience. There was also a boost of attention span but did not last for long. Overtime, I hope to gain the full benefits of meditation.

What new things have you learnt about meditation?

I found that meditation helped me to ‘let go’. Letting go of everything in the past, present and future and just exist for a few minutes. To be happy in your own presence and gain fulfilment out of it. A feeling you could only describe when experienced firsthand.

Did any of the suggested meditation techniques stand out to you?

Mindfulness meditation stood out to me the most as I think improvement in that area would be beneficial. To bring complete attention to the present experience on a moment to moment basis is something I found quite interesting.
