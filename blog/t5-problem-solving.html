Problem Solving

What problem did you get blocked on and what techniques did you use to solve it?

The problem I got blocked on was a JavaScript code for updating count whenever a dot is turned blue, green and invisible. I mainly used google to find codes that are similar and understand it from a different perspective.

How did you feel throughout the process?

I felt frustrated that I could not solve this code after trying for an hour, but also felt relieved once I solved the problem.

What did you learn?

I learned to deal with my emotions better by telling my self how I feel when solving a problem. For instance “I feel frustrated trying to write this code”, this acknowledges my emotions and calms them down.

How confident are you in ‘asking your peers for help’?

Soon, I will be asking my peers for help because sometimes it is also better to get other people perspective of your problem. Overall, I am confident in asking my peers if I’m in need of help.

How confident are you in ‘asking coaches for help’?

Relating to the method above, I am confident in asking coaches if I’m in need of help.
